About The Game

Carrot Chase is a delightful 3D adventure game where you control a bunny on a mission to collect carrots in various themed environments. With simple controls and challenging gameplay, it's perfect for players of all ages!

The game features five unique levels, each with its own theme and challenges. Race against time to collect all carrots before the timer runs out!

Developed by Srikrishna Senthilkumar, Carrot Chase combines colorful 3D graphics with engaging gameplay for a fun gaming experience.

Game Levels

Level 1: Meadow

A beautiful meadow filled with California poppies. Collect 3 carrots as you hop through this beginner-friendly level.

Level 2: Lily Pond

Navigate a serene lily pond environment. Find 5 carrots hidden among the lilies and water features.

Level 3: Shipwreck

Explore an underwater shipwreck with realistic ship hull and tattered sails. Collect 7 carrots in this mysterious setting.

Level 4: Rain Forest

Journey through a lush Amazon-like rainforest with dense vegetation. Find 9 carrots hidden among the trees.

Level 5: Jurassic Park

The ultimate challenge! Collect carrots while avoiding chasing dinosaurs in this prehistoric-themed level.
